ABOUT US

MVCS enables you to take your computer science skills to the next level. If you've taken an MV course such as Java or APCS but want to go to the next level , then MVCS is for you. Whether you want to become a web developer, a game designer, an algorithmic programmer, or pursue other fields, there is something for you in MVCS.

Awesome Lectures

Each week on Wednesday in room E203, we lecture on a new and interesting topic, ranging from Cryptography to Spam Filtering. We optimize for topics that are outside-the-box (you won't ever see them on a CollegeBoard test), have practical applications, yet are easy enough to write code for right off the bat without needing much prior experience. All these lectures fit into a broad monthly topic.

Monthly Contests

Every month, we have monthly contests based on the concepts we cover in Lecture Day. Prove that you've been paying attention by applying these concepts to real life problems! You can win prizes, including Ipads and Kindles, for winning these contests.You also can demonstrate to colleges that you truly understand computer science.

This Month's Contest: Wikipedia Freefall Opens Oct. 31st!

Algorithm Day

Do you want to specialize ? If you are interested in algorithms, such as sorting and binary search, or if you like math, considering attending Algorithm Day (date TBD) . There, we teach you how to train for prestigious computing competitions such as USACO and ACSL. We've had an impressive success rate so far, producing 9 USACO Silver medalists and 2 USACO Gold medalists (out of about 200 worldwide). You will be coached by former USACO nationwide finalist Nikhil Cheerla.

Creative Day

Alternatively, attend Creative Day if you want to focus on careers such as web development or app development. In Creative Day, you can get Java or APCS homework help, work on monthly contests, or learn about development. Our very own Mahesh Murag will teach about concepts such as GitHub, HTML/CSS/JS, multi-platform app development, and acing coding interviews. In addition, in Creative Day we form teams in order to attend hackathons together. If you want to learn, network, or simply chill, Creative Day is the place for you.
